# Understanding HBAR: Hedera's Cryptocurrency

HBAR is the **native cryptocurrency** of the Hedera Hashgraph network. It is used for **transaction fees, network security, and staking**.

## Why is HBAR Unique?

- **Fast Transactions**: HBAR transactions settle in seconds.
- **Low Fees**: Costs just a fraction of a cent per transaction.
- **Eco-Friendly**: Uses **proof-of-stake** instead of energy-intensive mining.
- **Security**: Helps secure the Hedera network against attacks.

HBAR plays a critical role in **powering the Hedera ecosystem** and enabling efficient, low-cost decentralized applications.
